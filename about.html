OUR STORY

A Legacy Built On Trust Since 1986

For nearly four decades, Souviks has supplied genuine and aftermarket commercial vehicle spare parts to workshops, retailers and fleet operators across India.

What began as a local spare parts business has grown into a trusted name known for reliability, product knowledge and consistent customer support.

Today we stock filters, bearings, clutch parts, brake components, lubricants, electricals, engine parts and thousands of related products for trucks and buses.
